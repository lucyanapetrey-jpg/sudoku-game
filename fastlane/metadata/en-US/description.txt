Sudoku Summer is a classic 9x9 number puzzle game for players who enjoy a relaxing daily challenge.

FEATURES
- Daily Puzzle: a fresh puzzle every day with a +50 coins reward on completion
- Three difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, Hard
- Daily Login Streak: increasing rewards for consecutive days (up to 150 coins on day 7)
- Coin system: earn coins by playing and spend them in the in-game store
- In-game store with four coin packs
- "Remove Ads" in-app purchase

HOW TO PLAY
Classic Sudoku rules: fill the 9x9 grid so that each row, each column and each 3x3 box contains the digits 1 through 9 with no repetition. Tap a cell, then pick a number from the keypad.

OPTIMIZED FOR iPHONE
Responsive interface with precise touch controls. Works offline after first launch.

DESIGN
Summer-inspired palette: shades of blue, orange and green. Clean typography for long, comfortable play sessions.

Made in Romania by Summer Smile Studio.
